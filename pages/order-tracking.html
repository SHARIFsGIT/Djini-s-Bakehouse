Frequently Asked Questions

For standard delivery, we typically deliver within 2-3 hours of order placement. Express delivery is available for a small additional fee and delivers within 1 hour. During peak times, delivery may take slightly longer. You can always track your order in real-time using our order tracking system.

We strive to deliver all orders on time, but occasionally delays may occur due to traffic, weather conditions, or high volume of orders. If your order is significantly delayed beyond the estimated delivery time, please contact our customer service team, and we'll make it right. Depending on the circumstances, we may offer compensation such as a discount on your next order.

Orders can be modified or canceled up to 15 minutes after placing them. After that time, your order may already be in preparation, and changes or cancellations might not be possible. To make changes or request a cancellation, please call our customer service line immediately at (555) 123-4567.

We take great pride in our products and aim for perfection with every order. If you're not completely satisfied with your order, please contact us within 2 hours of delivery. We'll gladly replace any items that don't meet our quality standards or offer a refund or store credit.
