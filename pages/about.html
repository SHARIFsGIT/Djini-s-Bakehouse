How It All Began

Djini's Bakehouse was born from a simple passion - creating delicious baked goods that bring joy to people's lives. Founded in 2015 by Master Baker Diana "Djini" Johnson, our bakery started as a small corner shop with just three signature items.

Diana's journey began in her grandmother's kitchen, where she learned the secrets of traditional baking. After formal training at the prestigious Paris Culinary Institute and working in renowned bakeries across Europe, she returned home with a vision to blend classic techniques with innovative flavors.

What started as a small operation has grown into the beloved bakery you know today, but our commitment remains the same: crafting exceptional baked goods with the finest ingredients, made fresh daily with attention to detail and a sprinkle of love.

2015

The Beginning

Diana opens the first Djini's Bakehouse location with her signature sourdough bread, croissants, and chocolate chip cookies.

Our Mission

To create moments of joy through exceptional baking, while nurturing our community and honoring traditional craftsmanship.

At Djini's Bakehouse, we believe in the power of good food to bring people together. Each day, we strive to create baked goods that not only taste exceptional but also create meaningful connections and memories for our customers.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Djini's Bakehouse.

Quality Ingredients

We believe great baking starts with great ingredients. We source locally whenever possible and use only premium, natural ingredients with no artificial preservatives.

Community First

We're proud to be part of our local community. We support local farmers, participate in community events, and donate leftover baked goods to local food banks.
