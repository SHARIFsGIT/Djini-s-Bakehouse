Ordering

For custom orders, we recommend placing your order at least 48 hours in advance. For larger orders or special occasion cakes, we suggest 3-7 days notice to ensure we can accommodate your request and have adequate time for preparation.

For wedding cakes and catering packages, please contact us at least 2-3 weeks in advance to discuss your specific requirements and schedule a tasting appointment.

Orders can be modified or canceled up to 24 hours before the scheduled pickup or delivery time. For custom orders, cancellations made less than 48 hours in advance may be subject to a cancellation fee.

To modify or cancel your order, please contact our customer service team as soon as possible at (555) 123-4567 or email orders@djinisbakehouse.com.

For bulk orders or catering for events, please fill out our Custom Order Form or contact us directly at (555) 123-4567.

We offer special packages and pricing for corporate events, weddings, and parties. Please allow at least 7-10 days notice for bulk orders to ensure availability.

Delivery

Yes, we offer delivery within a 15-mile radius of our bakery. Delivery fees start at $5.99 and may vary based on distance and order size. We also offer express delivery options for an additional fee.

For orders outside our delivery range, we partner with third-party delivery services that can bring our products to your doorstep.

Once your order is confirmed and out for delivery, you'll receive a text message or email with tracking information. You can also track your order in real-time through our mobile app or by visiting the Order Tracking page on our website.

If you have any questions about your delivery, please contact our customer service team at (555) 123-4567.

If you select contactless delivery, our driver will leave your order at your door and send you a confirmation photo. For regular deliveries, if no one is available to receive the order, our driver will call the provided phone number.

If we cannot reach you, we'll attempt to find a safe place to leave your order or return it to our store where you can pick it up later. In some cases, a redelivery fee may apply.

For perishable items, we recommend ensuring someone is available to receive the delivery or selecting a specific delivery time when you'll be home.

Products

We offer some gluten-free and nut-free options, which are prepared in a separate area of our kitchen. However, our facility does process products containing common allergens such as wheat, dairy, eggs, soy, and nuts. Please inform us of any allergies when placing your order, and we'll do our best to accommodate your needs.

For detailed allergen information about specific products, please contact us directly or visit our store to speak with our staff.

Our products are baked fresh daily and contain no preservatives. For optimal freshness, we recommend:

  • Bread: Consume within 2-3 days, store in a paper bag at room temperature
  • Pastries: Best consumed within 1-2 days
  • Cakes: Can be kept for 3-4 days in the refrigerator
  • Cookies: Stay fresh for 3-5 days in an airtight container

Most of our products can be frozen for longer storage. For best results, wrap them tightly in plastic wrap and place in a freezer bag before freezing.

Yes, we offer a selection of vegan and dairy-free products. Our vegan menu includes bread, muffins, cookies, and select pastries. We use plant-based alternatives to replace dairy ingredients.

For our complete selection of vegan and dairy-free options, please visit our Products page and filter by dietary preferences.

Payment

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay
  • Cash (for in-store purchases and cash on delivery)

For corporate orders and catering, we also accept company checks and offer invoicing options.

We take great pride in our products and want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, please contact us within 24 hours of receiving it.

For quality issues or errors in your order, we offer a full refund or replacement. Please keep your receipt and, if possible, take a photo of the product in question.

For our complete refund policy, please visit our Refund Policy page.

Yes, we offer both physical and digital gift cards in various denominations. Gift cards can be purchased online or in-store and never expire.

Digital gift cards are sent immediately via email, while physical gift cards can be picked up in-store or mailed to the recipient (additional shipping fees may apply).

To purchase a gift card, visit our Gift Cards page or ask our staff during your next visit.

Account

Creating an account is simple and free. Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top navigation bar or visit our Sign Up page. You'll need to provide your name, email address, and create a password.

Having an account allows you to:

  • Track your orders
  • Save favorite products
  • Store delivery addresses
  • Save payment methods securely
  • Earn and redeem loyalty points

Our loyalty program rewards you for every purchase. For every $1 spent, you earn 10 points. Points can be redeemed for discounts, free products, or exclusive offers.

Loyalty tiers:

  • Silver: 0-500 points (standard earning rate)
  • Gold: 501-2000 points (earn 1.2x points, birthday treat)
  • Platinum: 2001+ points (earn 1.5x points, birthday treat, early access to seasonal items)

Points expire after 12 months of inactivity. To view your current points balance, visit your Account page.

If you forgot your password, follow these steps:

  1. Go to the Login page
  2. Click on "Forgot Password"
  3. Enter your email address
  4. Check your email for a password reset link
  5. Click the link and follow the instructions to create a new password

If you don't receive the password reset email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or contact our customer support for assistance.
