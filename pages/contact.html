Frequently Asked Questions

For custom orders, we recommend placing your order at least 48 hours in advance. For larger orders or special occasion cakes, we suggest 3-7 days notice to ensure we can accommodate your request and have adequate time for preparation.

Yes, we offer delivery within a 15-mile radius of our bakery. Delivery fees start at $5.99 and may vary based on distance and order size. We also offer express delivery options for an additional fee.

For orders outside our delivery range, we partner with third-party delivery services that can bring our products to your doorstep.

We offer some gluten-free and nut-free options, which are prepared in a separate area of our kitchen. However, our facility does process products containing common allergens such as wheat, dairy, eggs, soy, and nuts. Please inform us of any allergies when placing your order, and we'll do our best to accommodate your needs.

For detailed allergen information about specific products, please contact us directly.

Orders can be modified or canceled up to 24 hours before the scheduled pickup or delivery time. For custom orders, cancellations made less than 48 hours in advance may be subject to a cancellation fee. Please contact our customer service team as soon as possible if you need to make changes to your order.

Yes, we offer catering services for various events, including corporate functions, weddings, birthday parties, and other special occasions. Our catering menu features a selection of our most popular items, and we can also create custom menus based on your preferences and dietary requirements.

Please contact us at least two weeks in advance for catering requests, and we'll work with you to create the perfect menu for your event.
